# Cheating Moth

Cheat at cards to win the game, but be careful, the Guard Bug is watching.

## Preparation

These steps should be done before the game is introduced to new players.

* Prune the deck down to just the grey and Moth (purple) cards.

## Setup & Explanation

I know that some of these rules are different to the core ruleset, but I think they make the game more fun for new players.

1. Shuffle the deck.
1. Place the Guard Bug (green) card face-up near the top of the deck.
1. Deal 7 cards to each player.
1. Tell the Guard Bug to leave the green card in front of them.
1. Place the remaining cards face-down in a draw pile.
1. Read "How to Play", stopping without explaining "Action cards".
1. State that the Guard Bug starts the game by turning over the top card, and this is exactly as if they had played it.
1. **Play!**

## Winning

The player who plays their last card, so emptying their hand, wins the game.
Yes, "play" not "cheat", you may not win by cheating away your final card.

## Someone was too good at this game...

... so you need a way to slow down front runners.

* Read the "Spider" rules from the "Action cards" section.
* Show everyone the Spider (yellow) cards.
* Shuffle the Spider (yellow) cards into the deck.

## The game ended way too quickly...

... so you need to put more cards into everyone's hands.

* Read the "Ant" rules from the "Action cards" section.
* Show everyone the Ant (blue) cards.
* Shuffle the Ant (blue) cards into the deck.

## The game still ends too quickly...

... so you need to start with more cards in everyone's hands.
If you've added most of the coloured cards, or you've less than 5 players:

* Deal 8 cards to each player.

## We need better distractions...

... so you need to add more visual noise and demands upon attention.

* Read the "Cockroach" rules from the "Action cards" section.
* Show everyone the Cockroach (orange) cards.
* Shuffle the Cockroach (orange) cards into the deck.

## The game needs even more distractions...

... so you need even more visual noise, movement and chaos‽

* Read the "Mosquito" rules from the "Action cards" section.
* Check even is okay with these rules.
* Show everyone the Mosquito (pink) cards.
* Shuffle the Mosquito (pink) cards into the deck.
